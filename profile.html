Jason Greenberg

Musician / Artist / Developer

Originally from New York, Jason moved to Florida in 1998 and immediately joined the South Florida technology industry, developing creative and interactive websites. Jason brings a background of graphic arts and client services to all of his positions within the industry. Jason has held application development positions and worked with several companies, including TEK GROUP Int., working on their award-winning Online Newsroom Software. Some of Jason’s past clients have included Toyota, Starbucks, Dunkin’ Donuts, Cracker Barrel, The Palm Beach County Sheriffs Office, and Office Depot.

Jason currently resides in South Florida with his wife and children.
